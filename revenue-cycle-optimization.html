Revenue Cycle Management

Scottline Healthcare Solutions offer complete collection of cloud-based RCM solutions helps in maximizing the revenue and productivity while reducing AR days and write-offs. Scottline Healthcare Solution's RCM solutions are always up-to-date with changing government regulations, payer policies, and patient payment models. And they all work together through a single, easy-to-use web-based portal for higher productivity and faster training.

    Our REVENUE CYCLE MANAGEMENT is ideal solves all these problems:
  • Revenue leakage and write-offs
  • Rejected claims and slow payments
  • Cash-flow issues and excessive AR days
  • Difficulty collecting from patients
  • High billing costs
  • ICD-10 and Meaningful Use mandates
  • The need to improve staff productivity
  • Inefficiencies in your RCM systems
